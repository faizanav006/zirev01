Common Questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

What makes an independent broker better? +
Unlike agents tied to one company, we shop dozens of carriers simultaneously. You get unbiased recommendations and better rates — because we work for you, not the insurer.
How fast can I get a quote? +
Most clients receive multiple competitive quotes within 24 hours. For standard auto and home, we often have options ready in under an hour.
Can I bundle multiple types of insurance? +
Absolutely — and you should. Bundling home, auto, and other policies typically saves clients 15–25%. Our brokers will show you exactly how much bundling saves you.
Does Zeen handle claims? +
Yes. We advocate for you throughout the claims process, liaise with your insurer, and make sure your claim is handled fairly and quickly.
What is group insurance and who qualifies? +
Group insurance is offered through your employer, union, or association. Members receive exclusive rates unavailable to the public. Check if your organization is one of our 150+ partners.
Which states and provinces do you cover? +
We are licensed across the United States including Virginia, California, Texas, Florida, New York, Nevada, Oregon, Washington, Georgia, and many more. Contact us to confirm your state.
